It's the solution to having limited space on cloud storage sites. Now you can own the storage instead of renting from Dopbox or One drive.


What is it?

Memory

Phase 1 is to give each and every home memory, The ability for your home to remember and work with all your files

Automation

Phase 2 is the ability to connect you homes smart devices to the computer such as speakers and printers allowing you to control you home from anywhere.

Speech

Phase 3 is to give you the ability to contorl your home through voice commands.like siri and Alexa


Designed To Excel
